FAQ

Questions small business owners search before using AI

What is the best first AI workflow for a small business?

Start with customer communication. Email replies, review responses, quote follow-ups, and support summaries are frequent, visible, and easy to measure.

Can AI replace staff?

Most small businesses should treat AI as a workflow assistant, not a replacement plan. The best use cases remove repetitive drafting, summarizing, formatting, and research work.

When should this site apply for Google AdSense?

Apply after publishing a useful set of original pages, adding About, Contact, Privacy Policy, and Terms pages, and getting consistent organic traffic. Affiliate and template revenue can start earlier.
