How to Write Job Descriptions with AI

Small teams cannot afford unclear hiring. AI can turn rough role notes into a cleaner job description, but the owner still needs to define what success looks like.

Start with outcomes

Before asking AI to write a job post, list the three outcomes the new hire must create in the first 90 days. This keeps the description focused on useful work instead of vague traits.

Ask AI for interview questions

After the job description, ask for practical interview questions that test real situations. For example, a customer service role should include a complaint response task, not just personality questions.

Check for accuracy and fairness

Review requirements carefully. Remove unnecessary degrees, vague personality demands, and anything that does not match the role. When hiring rules matter, get professional advice for your location.
