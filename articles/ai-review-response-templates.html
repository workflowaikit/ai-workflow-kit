What to check before publishing

  • Names and order details are correct.
  • The reply does not reveal private customer information.
  • The tone matches your brand.
  • The response does not promise refunds or outcomes unless approved.

Build a review response library

Save your best responses by category: thanks, delay, damaged item, booking issue, refund request, staff compliment, and misunderstanding. Over time, AI becomes faster because your examples become clearer.
