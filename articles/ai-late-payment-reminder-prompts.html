Protect the relationship

The best reminder is clear without sounding irritated. Before sending, check that the invoice details, contract terms, payment link, and recipient are correct.

Create a reminder sequence

  1. Friendly reminder two to three days after due date.
  2. Direct follow-up after seven to fourteen days.
  3. Final internal review before escalation.
