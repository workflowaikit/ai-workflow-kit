Repurpose instead of creating from zero

One customer question can become a short article, a FAQ, a Google Business Profile post, an email paragraph, and a social caption. This is how a small team stays consistent.

Keep it realistic

Start with one article and two short posts per week. A calendar that you can follow is better than an ambitious plan that gets ignored after ten days.
