## Chosen theme

Small business AI workflows, templates, and ROI tools for English-speaking business owners.

## Why this theme

- 2026 research signals show small businesses are adopting AI but still need practical training and implementation guidance.
- The audience has commercial intent: owners search for tools, templates, automations, and software comparisons.
- The site can monetize before Google AdSense through affiliate links and paid templates.
- The topic supports many long-tail SEO pages instead of depending on one competitive keyword.
